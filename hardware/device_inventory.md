# Device Inventory

The system integrates over 40 IoT devices including switches, sensors, cameras, and media devices.

Zigbee Devices
- smart switches
- door sensors
- window sensors
- motion sensors
- tilt sensor
- smart outlets

WiFi Devices
- TP-Link Kasa dimmer switches
- IR blasters
- Reolink cameras
- Ecobee thermostat

Media Devices
- AirPlay audio receiver
- home theater system
